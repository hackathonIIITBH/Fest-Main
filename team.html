Message from the Director, IIIT Bhagalpur


Good ideas are always crazy until they’re not!!! – Elon Musk

Indian Institute of Information Technology (IIIT) Bhagalpur is proud to present ENयुगMA the Annual Techno-Cultural Festival of the year 2021-22 with tag line “The Psyche of Unity”. Yes, it is the time of the year where the competent and confident young minds get united to showcase their innovative and technical ideas. The goal of this festival is to raise awareness of how the innovations keep getting better and better every day and make sure every mind is adept with today’s ideas.

This year’s festival calls out to every student to participate in the fest and showcase their extraordinary skills, be it a simple or a crazy idea.

The festival will showcase a diverse array of compelling projects and will organize many other special events. So, if you need a break from your everyday classes or the workload pressure, do not miss the fun! You may be stunned to find how much there is to learn and also meet new and exciting people.

A huge shout to our amazing team who are devoted to make this Festival a Memory.

I look forward to meeting you people in the Festival!!

Prof. Arvind Choubey
Director,
IIIT Bhagalpur

Message from Fest-Coordinator, IIIT Bhagalpur


I, on behalf of the Indian Institute of Information Technology (IIIT) Bhagalpur, am happy to announce that the time has come for the yearly festival, Enyugma: A Techno-Cultural Fest. The festival has been organized from 18th to 23rd October 2021. We are excited to provide you with an enriching and memorable time.

The festival will organize a variety of special events and loads of fun. There will be technical contests like Bit-by-Bit Hackathons, Code-Ignite, Codes & Volts, etc, and many more. From the cultural side, there will be contests on music, photography, animation, dance, art, poetry, workshops, and so much more. Love playing BGMI or Clash of Clans, we have a contest for the gamers too. Come and join us to compete, and just have the fun of your life. I invite every student to come and participate in the festival, not just to learn but to enjoy.

Take a break and enjoy the moments. We are determined to make this festival a memory but it would be possible only if you come and join us.

I look forward to meeting you people at the festival.

Dr. Prakash Ranjan
Fest Coordinator,
IIIT Bhagalpur

An entire gamut of activities is organized by Enyugma to unleash the talent that exudes from the budding student managers. Message from the Coordinator, Sports Affairs, IIIT Bhagalpur


Enyugma as the Annual Techno-Cultural Fest of IIIT Bhagalpur shall be virtual playground for online Gamers.
