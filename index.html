ENYUGMA is a celebration of the massive diversity and unity that characterises our country. We intend to provide an enriching experience to the participants by challenging their tech-cognizance, socio-cultural, managerial and leadership skills through this fest. It consists of exciting events, competitions and workshops featuring experts in related fields, focusing on domains like blockchain, machine learning, theatre and animation that recognise the role of the challenges faced by society in our everyday indulgences"

Events

Technical


These Events are Planned for the tech-savvy youth by entrepreneurs and tech gurus.

A set of workshops, hackathons and competitions to make the techies think out of the box and expand their horizons.

These Events Aims to inspire and empower the Technoids to create something new and innovate, and experience a wide spectrum of modern technology and connect with other technophiles from across the country.

Participate in whatever appeals to you, and let the fest speak to you!

Cultural


The Cultural Board, IIIT Bhagalpur aims to unite and reinvent a healthy working environment for everyone to express their creativity and love for their extracurricular talents.

The events hosted include Dance, Singing, Music, Debate, Poetry, Photography, Animation, Video Editing, and Art Completions.

Apart from the competitions, a Treasure Hunt will be hosted for everyone to unleash their inner Sherlock Holmes.

Workshops will be hosted to enhance your artistic and expressionistic skills.

Last but not the least, a Musical Night will be hosted with live music and to give everyone a hub to hang around and socialize.
